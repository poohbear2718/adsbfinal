Ingredients

3/4 cup balsamic vinegar
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1/4 cup tightly packed fresh basil leaves
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2 tablespoons olive oil
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 garlic clove, minced
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1/2 teaspoon salt
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8 plum tomatoes
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves

Directions

Step 1

For marinade, place first five ingredients in a blender. Cut four tomatoes into quarters and add to blender; cover and process until blended. Halve remaining tomatoes for grilling.

Step 2

In a bowl, combine chicken and 2/3 cup marinade; refrigerate, covered, 1 hour, turning occasionally. Reserve remaining marinade for serving.

Step 3

Place chicken on an oiled grill rack over medium heat; discard marinade remaining in bowl. Grill chicken, covered, until a thermometer reads 165Â°, 4-6 minutes per side. Grill tomatoes, covered, over medium heat until lightly browned, 2-4 minutes per side. Serve chicken and tomatoes with reserved marinade.
